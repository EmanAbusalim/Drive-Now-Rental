Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to rent a car?

To rent a car, you typically need:

  • A valid driver’s license.
  • A credit or debit card in your name.
  • Proof of identity.
  • To meet the minimum age requirement.

Can I rent a car without a credit card?

Most car rental companies require a credit card for security purposes. However, some may accept debit cards with additional documentation or a deposit.

What is the minimum age to rent a car?

The minimum age to rent a car is usually 21 years old. However, some companies may charge a "young driver fee" for drivers under 25.

Can I add an additional driver to my rental?

Yes, you can usually add an additional driver for a small fee. The additional driver must also meet the rental company’s requirements (e.g., valid license, minimum age).

What types of cars are available for rent?

We offer a wide range of vehicles, including:

  • Economy cars (for budget-friendly travel).
  • Sedans (for comfort and style).
  • SUVs (for families or off-road trips).
  • Luxury cars (for a premium experience).

Is insurance included in the rental price?

Basic insurance is often included, but it may have limitations. We recommend purchasing additional coverage (e.g., collision damage waiver or theft protection) for full peace of mind.

Can I cancel or modify my reservation?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your reservation free of charge up to 24 hours before your rental period begins. Check our cancellation policy for more details.

What happens if I return the car late?

Late returns may incur additional charges. Please contact us if you anticipate being late to avoid extra fees.

Can I rent a car one-way?

Yes, we offer one-way rentals. Additional fees may apply depending on the drop-off location.

What should I do if I have an accident or breakdown?

In case of an accident or breakdown:

  1. Contact emergency services if necessary.
  2. Notify us immediately.
  3. Follow the instructions provided in your rental agreement.

Are there any mileage limits?

Most rentals come with unlimited mileage. However, some special offers or vehicle types may have mileage restrictions. Check our mileage policy for details.

Can I rent a car for someone else?

The primary driver must be the person who made the reservation and signed the rental agreement. Additional drivers can be added for a fee.

Do you offer long-term rentals?

Yes, we offer discounts for long-term rentals (e.g., weekly or monthly). Contact us for more information.

What fuel policy do you follow?

Our standard fuel policy is full-to-full:

  • The car is provided with a full tank of fuel.
  • You must return it with a full tank to avoid refueling charges.

Can I rent a car if I’m visiting from another country?

Yes, international travelers can rent a car with a valid driver’s license from their home country. An International Driving Permit (IDP) may be required in some cases.

How do I extend my rental period?

To extend your rental, contact us before your current rental period ends. Extensions are subject to availability and additional charges.

Are child seats available?

Yes, we offer child seats for an additional fee. Please request them at the time of booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard) and, in some cases, debit cards. Cash payments are generally not accepted.

Can I pick up or drop off the car outside of business hours?

After-hours pickups or drop-offs may be available at select locations. Contact us in advance to arrange this.

How do I get a receipt for my rental?

You can request a receipt at the time of return, or it will be emailed to you automatically.

Still Have Questions?

If you have any other questions, feel free to contact us. We're here to help!
